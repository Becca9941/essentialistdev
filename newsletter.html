Each email will be short and snappy, containing the best bits of one of the following content categories:

  • Action plans (core steps only).
  • Tech journal (core techniques and the why behind them).
  • Non-tech (the best transferable advice from other disciplines).
  • Wellbeing (snappy tips about how to improve your life right now).

I love getting replies to these emails too, so feel free to respond if you'd like to share your ideas and thoughts too!
